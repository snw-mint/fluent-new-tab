Why choose Fluent New Tab?

Minimalist & Lightweight

Lightning fast performance with no visual clutter to slow down your browser.

Stay Productive

Keep your most important links and tools just one click away.

Easy Customization

Personalize shortcuts, widgets, and layouts effortlessly to match your workflow.

Adaptive Theme

Beautifully crafted for both light and dark modes, or set it to sync with your system.

Total Control

Disable any element you don't need. You can even clear everything to just enjoy your wallpaper.

Privacy First

No trackers, no telemetry. All your preferences and links are stored safely on your local device.

Core Features

Productivity Hub

Launch your favorite apps, websites, and perform instant searches directly from your new tab. Everything you need is seamlessly integrated.

  • App Launcher built-in
  • Organizing with folders

Immersive & Aesthetic

  • Light, Dark & System Theme Modes
  • Personalized greetings

Everyday Utilities
